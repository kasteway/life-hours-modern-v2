About LifeTime

LifeTime is designed to help you visualize how your daily choices accumulate over your lifetime. By understanding the long-term impact of your routine, you can make more informed decisions about how you spend your most precious resource - time.

The calculations are based on averages and assumptions that you can customize. Remember that life is unpredictable, and these are estimates meant to provide perspective, not precise predictions.

Our goal is to inspire you to reflect on your priorities and consider how you might want to adjust your daily habits to align with your values and aspirations.
