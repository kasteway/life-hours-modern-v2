About Life Hours

Life Hours is designed to help you visualize how your daily choices accumulate over your lifetime. By understanding the long-term impact of your routine, you can make more informed decisions about how you spend your most precious resource - time.

The calculations are based on averages and assumptions that you can customize. Remember that life is unpredictable, and these are estimates meant to provide perspective, not precise predictions.
